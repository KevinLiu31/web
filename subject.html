    Video Game

    The original pokemon red, blue, and green game was released in Japan on February 27, 1996. It was then released in the USA on September 28 1998 as Pokemon red and blue, with green not being released. The game came out for the Nintendo gameboy. It has sold 300 million copes world wide. The company game freak, has seen released 30 more games, across multiple hand held consoles. The basis of the game is simple, you are a 10 year old child, trying to be the very best pokemon trainer in the world. To do this you engage in pokemon battles, where you fight other people's pokemon with your pokemon. You will engage in larger gym battles where you will gain badges, with typically 8 badges in each game. When you have all 8, you will then fight the elite 4 and then the champion, to take the title of the very best. You start off with a choice of 1 out of 3 starter pokemon, and you catch more along the way. Games

    Anime

    The Pokemon anime was first release on April 1, 1997. It follows the main character Ash Ketchum, as he explore different regions, take on gym challenges and try to become a pokemon master. He starts off without one of the three kanto starters, but with a pikachu, because he slept in. On the way to the first gym, he met Misty, whose bike he destroyed, he then challenge the first gym, after he defeated the gym, the leader Brock came with Ash and Misty on their pokemon Journey. Through out the journey through kanto, Ash capture many pokemon and defeat all 8 gyms, and made the pokemon league, but sadly only placed top 16. The group then went to Jhoto, where another journey happen. Next is Honen, where Misty is replace with another female character May, and her little brother joined the journey. In the Honen region we were introduced to the pokemon beauty contest. At the end of the journey May and Max split with brock and Ash. Ash and Brook then went to the Sinoh region, where they meet Dawn who joined them. In this league, Ash placed top 4. Following which Ash went to the Unova region, and parted ways with Brock. There he was met with Iris and Cilan, who joined him, in his journey. He then went to Kalos where he was met with his childhood friend (and lover) Serena, and Clemont and his sister Bonnie joined as well. Following this, he went to alola, where he met a whole new cast of people, this season was different as there were no gym. After this, was pokemon Journey which is also very different as there is no selected region. Anime

    Trading Card

    The Pokemon Trading Card was introduced in 1996, with the base set. It came out with the orginal 151 pokemon. Now there are 74 sets to this day. The original pokemon set is now around 500 dollars. In March 24, 2011, the pokemon trading card online game was release. Pokemon trading card even have a tournaments around the world and internationally. Some of the card can be worth as much as 10,000 USD! The game is simple you each have a deck of 50 cards, you start off with a stage 0 pokemon and if you have it's evolution you can evolve it. At the start of your turn you draw a card. Each pokemon has two moves and a health. Your job is to get rid of your opponents pokemon. Attack cost energy, and you need to supply energy card to the pokemon. Trading Card
