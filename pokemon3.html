Pokemon has produce many products, the most popular of which are their toys and Trading card games.

TRADING CARD

The Pokemon Trading Card was introduced in 1996, with the base set. It came out with the orginal 151 pokemon. Now there are 74 sets to this day. The original pokemon set is now around 500 dollars. In March 24, 2011, the pokemon trading card online game was release. Pokemon trading card even have a tournaments around the world and internationally. Some of the card can be worth as much as 10,000 USD! The game is simple you each have a deck of 50 cards, you start off with a stage 0 pokemon and if you have it's evolution you can evolve it. At the start of your turn you draw a card. Each pokemon has two moves and a health. Your job is to get rid of your opponents pokemon. Attack cost energy, and you need to supply energy card to the pokemon. Trading Card Game

Toys

From Plushies to lunchboxes, pokemon has made a large amount of toys. Here we are looking through some of the most interesting toys. With the popularity of Funko and Pokemon, of course they will do a collaboration and bring us Pokemon funkos, they are perfect as collectibles. Pokemon also did a collaboration with legos to allow kids to build their own pokemon, these sets included charizard, pikachu, and gyarados. Pokemon has also produced life size pokeball toys, which lights up when thrown. Pokemon Plushies have been popular ever since the game release, these plushies can cost upwards of $5000 dollars!
